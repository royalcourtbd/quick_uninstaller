INTRO Introduction

Welcome to Quick Uninstaller ("we," "our," or "us"). Quick Uninstaller is an Android application developed by Amatullah Tech. that helps users view installed apps, search and sort app lists, open app details, launch apps, open Play Store pages, create shortcuts, and start uninstall actions for user-installed apps.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the application ("App"). By using Quick Uninstaller, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

01 Information We Collect

1.1 Installed App Information

Quick Uninstaller reads installed app information from your device so the App can provide its core app management features. This may include:

  • App name - the display name of an installed app
  • Package name - the Android package identifier of an installed app
  • Version name - the installed version of an app
  • App size - the approximate size of the installed app package
  • Install date - the first install time reported by Android
  • App icon - the icon shown in the app list
  • System app status - whether an app is a system app or a user-installed app
Core functionality notice: The App uses broad installed-app visibility because its main purpose is to show and manage installed apps. Quick Uninstaller does not silently uninstall apps; Android shows a confirmation screen before an uninstall action is completed.

1.2 Device Storage Information

The App reads device storage information, such as total and available storage, to show storage availability inside the app manager screen.

1.3 Preferences Stored on Your Device

The App may store simple local preferences, such as your selected sort option, so the app can remember your preferred view. These preferences are stored locally on your device.

1.4 Automatically Collected Information Through Firebase

The App may use Firebase services to improve reliability, understand basic usage, and deliver app notices or update information. Depending on the enabled Firebase services, information may include:

  • Crash logs and diagnostics collected by Firebase Crashlytics
  • App usage events such as app open events collected by Firebase Analytics
  • Device or other identifiers used by Firebase services
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token used for app notices or notifications if messaging is enabled

1.5 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require users to create an account.
  • We do not collect usernames or passwords.
  • We do not collect SMS message content.
  • We do not collect contact lists.
  • We do not sell personal data.

04 Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Data is Stored

  • On device: Installed app information is read from your device to show the app list. Local preferences may be stored on your device.
  • Firebase services: Analytics, crash reports, messaging tokens, and remote notice/update metadata may be processed by Firebase/Google services when those SDKs are enabled.

4.2 Security Measures

  • Network communication with Firebase and Google services uses encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections.
  • Uninstall actions use Android's standard confirmation flow.
  • The App hides itself from the uninstall list to reduce accidental self-removal.
  • System apps are not selectable for batch uninstall inside the App.

05 Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Control Uninstall Actions

Quick Uninstaller starts Android's standard uninstall flow. You remain in control and must confirm uninstall requests through Android before an app is removed.

5.2 Clear Local Data

5.3 Notifications

If notifications are enabled on your device, you can disable them at any time through Android notification settings.

5.4 Data Deletion Requests
